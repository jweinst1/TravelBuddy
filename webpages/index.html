Pick a Location

Select from thousands of locations big or small

Travel buddy lets you select any destination, big or small to plan your trip. You can pick multiple cities, streets, and even countries. Map it out and see where your heart takes you.

Pick a Time

Travel whenever you want

Travel buddy allows you to select your preferred time, arrival date, departure dates and more. We will match you up with other people who want to travel at the same time as you.

Pick a group

Or Start Your Own!

Once you have your time and places selected, check out who's going too! You can make your own group to travel with, or select one that is leaving soon. Travel Buddy puts meeting new people and exploring new places right in your hands.
